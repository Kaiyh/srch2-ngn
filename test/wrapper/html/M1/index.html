OmniPlaces is a location-based search Web site demonstrating the patent-pending search algorithm of Bimaple Technology Inc..

It's built on 17 million US business listings provided by Factual.com and a proprietary search engine. It has the following unique features:

+ Instant search: supporting "search as you type";

+ Error-tolerant search: finding relevant results even if there are typos in the search keywords;

+ High efficiency and scalability: returning relevant results on millions of records in milliseconds.

For each business list, the site also returns information from location-based sites such as CitySearch, Yelp, and FourSquare.

The backend technology is described in a white paper. For more information, please visit www.bimaple.com or email contact at bimaple.com.
